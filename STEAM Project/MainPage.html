Facts About Pythons

  1. They are cold-blooded, meaning they cannot control their body temperature
  2. Pythons eat 4-5 times per year
  3. Pythons kill thier prey by suffocating them
  4. Although they are mainly located on the ground or in the trees, pythons are excellent swimmers.

Python are very interesting creatures. They can be kept as pets. If you are thinking of adopting a python as a pet, make sure you adopt it from a trustworthy animal shelter. Sadly, some species of python are endangered due to poaching and animal trafficking.

Commonly-Known Species of Python and their Conservation Status

Species Conservation Status
Ball Python (Python regius) Least Concern
Burmese Python (Python bivittatus) Vulnerable
